Governor limits
---------------

Apex runtime engine strictly enforces limits to ensure that Apex code or processes don't monopolize shared resources

Different Governor limits in Salesforce
---------------------------------------

1. Per-transaction Apex limits (number of transactions, number of records in a transaction)
2. Force.com platform Apex limits
3. Static Apex limits (size of static pages)
4. Size-specific Apex limits (maximum number of characters for class or triggers)
5. Miscellaneous Apex limits (default timeout for http requests)
6. Email limits (number of emails sent per day)
7. Push notification limits (number of notifications sent per day, number of notification queued)
